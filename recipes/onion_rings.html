Description:

Onion rings are a great snack - often ordered as a side with burgers or pizza.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Onion
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Dry Bread Crumbs
  • Seasoned Salt

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Slice onion into rings
  3. Prepare breading station with three dishes. Flour, baking powder, and salt in the first dish. Milk and egg in the second dish. Bread crumbs in the third dish.
  4. Dip each onion ring into each dish, turning over.
  5. Deep fry
  6. Sprinkle with seasoning salt.
