Description:

Garlic Bread is a delicious side dish. Often served as a side with pizza, but can also be eaten on it's own.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Marjoram
  • Herbs
  • Black Pepper
  • Italian Bread

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Mix butter, cheese, garlic, marjoram, herbs, pepper into a bowl until combined.
  3. Spread butter on bread.
  4. Heat in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
